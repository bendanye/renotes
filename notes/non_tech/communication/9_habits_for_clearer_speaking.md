# 9 Habits for Clearer Speaking (I Wish I Knew Sooner)

## Delivery habits
- pause
- slow down, highlight
- declarativr statement

## Vocal habits
- warm up your voice
- nose breathing
- use more volume when speaking

## Cognitive habits
- finish 1 thought at a time
- framework (context, core, connect)
- analogy
