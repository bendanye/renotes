# Why Your 'Reliable' System Will Fail

## Post-incident review traps

Avoid these as they are less useful to get answers

1. Human Error

Attribute to just saying "is a human mistake"

2. Counterfactual reasoning
3. Normative Language 

Phrases like Should have, could have

4. Mechanistic Reasoning

## Is my {something} resilient?

Four senses of resilient:
1. Rebound
2. Robustness
3. Graceful extensibility
4. Sustained adaptability

Adaptive capacity!

Restarting server that solve problem does not mean is resilient
